The fast growing industry of online shopping has presented a lot of challenges for consumers to know about the quality of the product they are looking to buy. Consumers often look to seek the help of reviews to see how the product fares but they could often fall prey to the fabricated reviews made to either promote or degrade the said product. This research aims to provide a review verification system that uses machine learning techniques to identify genuine and relevant reviews. Furthermore, we aim to simplify the process for the end consumer by implementing a chatbot using Phi-3 model. This chatbot helps the consumer by providing answer to user queries by extracting the relevant reviews and summarizing it. We aim to significantly reduce the time for consumer to analyse the product before making a decision to buy. Finally, we provide a full frontend application which enhances the user’s interaction with our intelligent review system. Our solution aims to create an online shopping ecosystem which helps both consumers and sellers who are looking to build trust with consumers.
