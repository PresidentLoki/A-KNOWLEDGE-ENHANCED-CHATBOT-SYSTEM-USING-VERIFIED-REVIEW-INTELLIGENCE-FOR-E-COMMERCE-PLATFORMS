Review 2:
I am working from home, and due to this reason, I end up wearing these for over 8 hours continuously. The earpads are very comfortable and do not cause any sort of pain on your ears. The sound quality is great for both calls and playing games. The mic does not pick up sounds like a mechanical keyboard clicking and water pouring but clearly picks up any voice activity without any disturbances or extra noise. The connectivity is absolutely hassle-free. I use a Linux machine, and the wireless connectivity is not great over Bluetooth (for any device), but the USB connector makes it absolutely easy to use. The USB connector allows me to walk away for up to 15 meters without any disconnection. Bluetooth works hassle-free with other devices such as my mobile phone. I would say if you're in the market for a great pair of headphones that are of good build and sound quality while being light in weight, these are just perfect.I would prefer the price to be around 5k instead of 7.5k, based on the materials used, but sure.

Review 3:
So I researched for weeks and then I finally went ahead to buy this. I'll keep it extremely short. For this price range, I think this is the best wireless gaming headphone you could get in India. It is lightweight, the sound is great, the battery backup is extremely good. The only drawback is the mic, it has no noise cancellation at all, the person on the other end can literally hear everything in the background. If mic is not a game breaker for you, go ahead with it. Don't get me wrong, it's not like the mic is bad, like in an empty room without disturbance in the background, the mic works pretty well. Let's hope they bring out a new model with improved mic.
